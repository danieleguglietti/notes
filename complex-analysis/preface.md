# Preface

The course aims to find the way to analyse the **complex valued functions of complex variables** (from now on just _complex functions_). We already have discussed on the study of the behaviour of a real functions, we now want to extend the same tools on the complex as far as we can.

We'll see how to compute limits, when a complex function is differentiable and how to find its derivative and so on.
